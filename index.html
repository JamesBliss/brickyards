Brickyards Kennels and Grooming Salon is situated between Pembridge and Kington and close to Leominster and is surrounded by beautiful Herefordshire countryside. Welcoming guests from Hereford, Ludlow, Presteigne and the surrounding area.

Run by Josie and Kenny Desborough, Brickyards is a small licensed boarding kennels and Grooming Salon, having just twelve kennels of various sizes to suit individual dogs and family groups needs.

Brickyards kennels and grooming salon are set in a secure, walled courtyard. All the kennels have sleeping areas which are well insulated and heated for those colder spells and have individual covered runs, looking out onto the courtyard. Music and talk radio is played during the day.

All dogs are exercised four times per day. Dogs are lead walked on various walks in over 200 acres of adjoining orchards, through our direct access private gate and off lead in our secure paddocks, maybe playing with a ball or Frisbee if they wish. The individual exercise needs of our guests are catered for, to suit old and young alike.

Brickyards is inspected and licensed by Herefordshire Council and dogs are fully insured while they are with us.

Small print

All dogs must hold a current vaccination certificate, if you do not bring your certificate with you and we can’t verify it with your vet we can’t board your dog.

We don’t insist on kennel cough vaccination but please ask your vets advice.

Should we feel the need to call a vet for an existing health condition, all fees incurred are the owner’s responsibility.

All pets should be worm and flea free at the time of boarding.

Should any pet on arrival require treatment for internal or external parasites (fleas, worms etc) then a charge will be levied for this.

All medical and dietary requirements should be discussed prior to your pet boarding with us.

Dogs are insured while they are boarding with us, excluding fees in connection with any conditions that exist prior to boarding.

All pets requiring veterinary treatment will be seen by our local veterinary practice unless prior arrangements are made.

Whilst every possible attention is given to each pet boarded with us, it is done at the owners risk.

Pets from the same family who share, who we feel need to be separated in the interests of safety, will be separated and an additional charge will be levied. In such cases your emergency contact will be advised.

Current or ongoing problems with your dog’s health or temperament must be notified to us at the time of booking. We reserve the right to refuse any pet that we consider to be of an unreliable temperament, or sick at the time of booking or arrival.

Grooming:

Josie is trained to City and Guilds standards in Dog Grooming. We can offer this service during the dogs stay with us or as a separate appointment. Please phone for an appointment and prices. (Please note that if a dogs coat is severely matted it may be required to partially or fully clip off the dogs coat. We fully conform to the Animal Welfare Act and will not cause an animal any unnecessary suffering.)

Feeding:

We feed the dogs with a range of quality dry and tinned pet food. If your dog is on a special diet or you prefer to bring the dogs usual food in we are happy to feed this.

Medicine:

Should your dog be taking any medication, we will be happy to administer it. Please advise any treatment prior to your dog’s stay.
